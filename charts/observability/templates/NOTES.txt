1. Prometheus

   To port-forward Prometheus:

   kubectl port-forward -n {{ include "observability.namespace" . }} svc/{{ include "observability.fullname" . }}-prometheus 9090:{{ .Values.prometheus.service.port }}

   Then open: http://localhost:9090

   Example query:

   geth_workload_head_block_number

2. Grafana

   To port-forward Grafana:

   kubectl port-forward -n {{ include "observability.namespace" . }} svc/{{ include "observability.fullname" . }}-grafana 3000:{{ .Values.grafana.service.port }}

   Then open: http://localhost:3000

   Login with:
     user: {{ .Values.grafana.adminUser }}
     pass: {{ .Values.grafana.adminPassword }}
